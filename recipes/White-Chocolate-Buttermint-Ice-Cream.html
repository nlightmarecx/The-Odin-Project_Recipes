White Chocolate Buttermint Ice Cream

Description

"A few months ago, I had some of this buttermint ice cream at Jeni’s with Ashley. No… it’s not normally any sort of flavor that I’d usually get. It doesn’t have chocolate or caramel or bacon or sprinkles or peanut butter chunks. It doesn’t have ribbons of fudge or cubes of cake. But it just so happened to be one of the 14 flavors that I sampled before making my decision (typical)… and I couldn’t get it out of my head."

"This ice cream… I mean, it’s turning me into an ice cream person. It’s 40 thousand times better than those mints. And it’s not super minty – it has just a slight hint of mint and is so buttery and creamy – it literally melts in your mouth. No, really… SO BUTTERY AND CREAMY. Oh, and the fact that there are white chocolate flecks speckled through the ice cream? Yeah. I dieeeee."

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole milk,
  • One 1/2 tablespoons cornstarch,
  • 3 tablespoons cream cheese,
  • 5 ounces high-quality white chocolate, chopped

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, mix together 2 tablespoons of the milk with the cornstarch and whisk until smooth.
