White Chocolate

Description

"While it might not be the type of chocolate you want to chow down on in candy bar form, white chocolate is a perfect medium for dipping fruit or pretzels, fondue-style; as a foundation for chocolate bark; or for a decorative glaze on cakes and cookies."

"But to do any of those delicious things with white chocolate, you’ve got to melt it first.

How to melt a chocolate?

"Our recommendation is microwave. The microwave method is quick but hands-on. You’ll need to check the chocolate and stir in intervals to make sure it doesn’t overcook and burn. Depending on the power of your microwave, you may want to adjust the interval timing slightly. All microwaves are different, and you’ll learn the sweet spot for yours the more you use it.
